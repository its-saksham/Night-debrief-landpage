How it works
1

Talk, don't type

Open it before bed and say what happened. No blank page. No prompts to overthink. Just answer the questions.

2

It remembers so you don't have to

A notebook doesn't ask what happened to yesterday's plan. This does — every night, automatically, without you reading old pages.

3

It reads between the lines

Across entries, it catches what repeats — and says so, in plain language. No charts. Just the truth.

4

No streaks to break

Miss a night, skip a plan — it's just noted, not flagged. There's no score resetting to zero. Tomorrow starts clean either way.
